Paris Breakfast
If you think breakfast in Paris is still a croissant and a café crème, think again.
The past few years have seen a rise of breakfast-centric establishments all over the city that have menus packed with healthy options, like chia bowls and avocado toast (and less healthy options like cinnamon rolls and brioche sandwiches).
